# GRC Risk Register – Sample

## What this is
A sample cybersecurity risk register demonstrating how risks are identified, scored,
tracked, and mitigated in a Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) program.

## Why it matters
This project demonstrates:
- Risk identification and assessment
- Likelihood and impact scoring
- Control mapping and ownership
- Ongoing risk tracking

## Contents
- risk_register.csv – example risk register
- risk_matrix.png – likelihood vs impact heatmap (optional)

## Notes
This is a personal sample. No proprietary or employer data is included.
